Scenario: How do I resolve errors when subscribing through a marketplace?edit

If you encounter problems subscribing to Elasticsearch Service through one of the available marketplaces, it may be due to one of these two problems.

You are trying to subscribe using an existing Elastic Cloud email addressedit

If you already have an Elastic Cloud account, when you subscribe to Elasticsearch Service from a marketplace account you need to use an email address other than the one that is already associated with Elastic Cloud.

To continue using the email address for the marketplace, you can first change the primary email on your existing Elastic Cloud account, and then set up your new marketplace account.

Your marketplace account is already associated with a different Elastic Cloud email addressedit

Subscribing to Elasticsearch Service from a marketplace fails if the marketplace account is already associated with an existing Elastic Cloud email address. To resolve this, we recommend Getting help through the Elastic Support Portal.
