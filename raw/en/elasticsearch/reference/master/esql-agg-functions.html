ES|QL aggregation functionsedit

STATS ... BY support these functions:

  • AVG
  • COUNT
  • COUNT_DISTINCT
  • MAX
  • MEDIAN
  • MEDIAN_ABSOLUTE_DEVIATION
  • MIN
  • PERCENTILE
  • SUM
